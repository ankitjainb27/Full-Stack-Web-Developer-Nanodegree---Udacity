Daily Weather

Daily Weather will give you weather forecast for the next two weeks, with more details just a click away.
Details include High and low temperatures, humidity, Pressure and Wind speed.

More Features -

  • Only first time automatically your location will be picked up from GPS.
  • Internet is required just to refresh.
  • You will get notified by everyday weather.
  • You can change the location in the Settings, to the one you want by writing Name of the place or ZIP Code.
  • You can choose either Celsius or Fahrenheit Temperature units.
  • You can easily share weather with friends and family on Whatsapp, Facebook etc., from the details page.

Top Movies

Top movies will give you list of most popular, most rated and highest rated movies.
Along with movie name, the app also gives Movie description, Average Rating, Release date and YouTube link of its trailer.

More Features -

  • The app shows the movies in beautiful list, by individually colouring the container with a colour taken from movies cover photo
  • One can save movies to ones favourites by clicking on the heart and see them in one place in Favourites section.
  • One can include or exclude adult movies.

Singer's Track

In Singer's track you can search for singers and listen to their Top Tracks.
It shows list of Top Track of the singer, from which one can choose and play a track of their choice. It also shows movie name to which the song belongs.

More Features -

  • One can listen to the 30 seconds part of song. App uses Spotify to play the portion.
  • One can share song link of Spotify through all possible means such as facebook, Whatsapp, SMS etc.,
  • One can turn on and off the music player through notification drop down.
  • Works in landscape and portrait both modes.
